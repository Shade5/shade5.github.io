Computerized Tomography (CT) is a radiographic method for volumetric details in three dimensions. CT has been used almost entirely for medical purposes but over the past two decades, it has been used in non-destructive testing of industrial materials. The steps for generating a 3D model of an object are:

  • Scanning the object from specific angles with an X-ray
  • Reconstruction of layers using tomography
  • Filtering to remove reconstruction artifacts
  • Stacking layers to build the 3D structure
